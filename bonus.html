Parameshwar - Judge

Parameshwar is a Judge in Telangana. He started as a legal practitioner, then got promoted to Lawyer, then public prosecutor, afterwards as a Judge for session court and later to the current position as Chief Judge of High court.

  1. Wears Black Coat
  2. Stout
  3. Tall
  4. Has a mole on hand
  5. Right handed person
