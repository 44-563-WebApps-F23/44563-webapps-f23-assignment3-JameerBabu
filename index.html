Favorite Vacation Spot - Alampur

Alampur is my Favorite vacation spot as it an abode of Lord Bala Brahmeswara Swamy and Maa Jogulamba. It is the main attraction of our district. District name was modified based on this temple. It also has nice scenary with a river surrounding called Tungabhadra. It is the only place on earth where 9 shiva lingas are placed in single location. Next to the temple, there is an Archeological Department established office which preservers things related to ancient times.
